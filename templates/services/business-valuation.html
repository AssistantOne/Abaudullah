What is Business Valuation?

Business valuation is the process of determining the economic value of a business or company unit. It involves analyzing various factors including financial performance, market conditions, assets, liabilities, and future growth potential to arrive at an accurate assessment of the business's worth.

When Do You Need Business Valuation?

  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Determining fair value for buy/sell transactions
  • Investment Decisions: Assessing business worth for investment purposes
  • Financing: Establishing business value for loan applications and financing
  • Tax Planning: Valuation for tax purposes and estate planning
  • Strategic Planning: Understanding business value for strategic decision-making
  • Dispute Resolution: Independent valuation for legal and dispute resolution
